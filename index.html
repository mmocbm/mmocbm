What robot battle is

A robot battle, also known as robot combat or robot fighting, is a competitive event where robotic machines engage in combat against each other. These battles are typically held in controlled arenas or arenas specifically designed for robot combat. Participants design and build their own robots, equipped with various weapons and defenses, to compete against opponents with the goal of immobilizing or disabling their opponents' robots.

The Robot Battle organized by the department of Science and Technology of Uva Wellassa University of Sri Lanka is an exciting and competitive event that highlights the skills and innovations in the field of robotics. The department of Science and Technology, which is known for its focus and advancement on the field of science and technology, hosts this annual competition to bring together robotics enthusiasts from various educational institutions across Sri Lanka and beyond.

The Robot Battle aims to promote and encourage the development of robotic technology and its practical applications. It serves as a platform for participants to demonstrate their technical expertise, problem-solving abilities, and creativity in designing and building robots capable of engaging in thrilling battles.

The Robot Battle not only emphasizes the technical aspects of robotics but also fosters teamwork, collaboration, and critical thinking. It provides an opportunity for students to apply their theoretical knowledge in a practical setting and gain direct experience in engineering and design principles.

The event attracts a diverse range of participants, including students, researchers, and enthusiasts from various disciplines such as engineering, computer science, and robotics. Participants may come from different universities, research institutions, or even independent robotics clubs and organizations.

The Robot Battle organized by Uva Wellassa University of Sri Lanka serves as a significant platform for promoting the advancement of robotics in Sri Lanka. It encourages innovation, fosters a spirit of healthy competition, and cultivates a community of robotics enthusiasts who can contribute to the development of innovative technologies in the country.

Past Battles

As this is an annual event organized by the department of Science and Technology of Uva Wellassa University of Sri Lanka which started in 2016, The event itself has a history.
In 2016 the first robotic competition, “UWU Robot Battle 2016” was unveiled. The Robotic competition was open in two categories as ‘school challenge’ and ‘open challenge’ under two different tasks for those who were interested in robotics island wide. Undergraduate teams and any other teams were able to participate in the open challenge. This evaluated the advanced skills of participants that were required to complete a higher task in a challenging environment in diverse ways.
In 2018, the “UWU Robot Battle 2018” was successfully concluded for the second consecutive time. The competition was held under two categories; School and Open, where hundreds of young tech enthusiastic took part in the robotics extravaganza.
In 2019, Uva Wellassa University of Sri Lanka organized an exhilarating robot battle event “UWU Robot Battles 2019- Extra Edition” that highlighted the innovative engineering skills of its participants. The competition brought together students and enthusiasts who designed and built their own combat robots to engage in thrilling battles. The event provided a platform for displaying technical expertise, strategic thinking, and teamwork, making it a remarkable display of technological prowess and excitement.
